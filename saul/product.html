DisWatch (Distance Watch)

        This device measures the distance of the person wearing it to maintain the one-meter social distancing to other people close to it. DisWatch will alarm and activates the built in air purifier that helps in purifying the area around the person to keep the air clean to breathe and keep germs and viruses away and telling the person wearing it to observe the implemented one-meter social distancing. This device will surely become beneficial to the people who always engages in public for their work specially healthcare workers and to protect themselves acquiring the virus.
