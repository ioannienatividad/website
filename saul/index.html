Introduction


        This pandemic has made a lot of changes to lives of the people. Going out on public makes a lot of difference now and then. Following safety health protocols are important including the one-meter social distancing. But some people disregard these rules, and it seems like most of them are not afraid to get infected by Covid-19. Public markets, malls and public transportations are some of the places where social distancing should be strictly followed, but based on news reports the social distancing is always disregarded by some people that causes to the increasing numbers of Covid cases in the country.
